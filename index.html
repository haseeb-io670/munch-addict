About Global Munch

We bring the excitement of global snacking to your doorstep. Our curated collection features unique treats from countries around the world that you won't find in your local store.

Each month, we source new and exciting snacks from different regions to ensure you always have something new to discover.

Global Munch was founded in 2022 by two travel enthusiasts who missed the unique snacks they discovered during their international journeys. What started as importing treats for friends and family quickly turned into a full-fledged business when demand kept growing for these hard-to-find international snacks.
