Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, and more.

Ordering & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping within the US takes 3-5 business days after processing. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. International shipping times vary by location.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries! International shipping rates and estimated delivery times are calculated at checkout. Please note that any import duties or taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

What is your return policy?

Due to the nature of food products, we cannot accept returns. However, if you receive damaged products or if there's an issue with your order, please contact us within 7 days of delivery, and we'll make it right.

Is free shipping available?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all US orders over $50. International orders and expedited shipping options have additional fees calculated at checkout.

Wholesale & Other Questions

Do you offer wholesale options?

Yes, we work with retailers across the country! If you're interested in carrying Global Munch products in your store, please email us at wholesale@globalmunch.com for our wholesale catalog and pricing information.

Can I find your products in stores?

Yes! Our products are available in select specialty stores, candy shops, and gift boutiques across the US. Check our "Stores" page for a list of retailers, or contact us to find the nearest location to you.

How should I store my Exotic Snacks?

Our candies are best stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. While refrigeration isn't necessary, it can help preserve freshness in very warm climates. Always reseal the package after opening to maintain optimal texture and flavor.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Send us an email and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
