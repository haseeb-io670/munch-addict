Product Description

Our Spicy Gummy Bears are the perfect combination of sweet and heat! These adorable bears start with the fruity sweetness you expect from gummy candies, but then surprise you with a warming chili kick that gradually builds as you enjoy them.

We use a proprietary blend of chili peppers to create just the right balance - enough heat to excite your taste buds without overwhelming them. Each bear is infused with our special chili extract, ensuring a consistent and enjoyable heat experience in every piece.

These are perfect for spice enthusiasts, adventurous snackers, or as a conversation-starting treat at your next gathering. Challenge your friends to see who can handle the most bears in one sitting!

Each 4oz bag contains a colorful mix of approximately 25-30 bears in various fruit flavors, including strawberry, orange, lemon, grape, and lime.
