About Chilli Bomba

We bring a spicy kick to your favorite candies. Our premium handcrafted treats are made with the finest ingredients and just the right amount of heat.

All products are made in small batches to ensure quality and freshness.

Chilli Bomba was founded in 2022 by two candy enthusiasts with a passion for spice. What started as kitchen experiments quickly turned into a full-fledged business when friends couldn't get enough of our unique creations.
